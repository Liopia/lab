Murmur Signature Builder

Once you have filled in your details below hilight the resulting signature, then copy and paste it into your email client.

If you use Apple Mail the signature will look bad when you paste it into the signature area. Please ensure that the "Always match my default message font" is unchecked before saving and then restart the application.

Hilight tip: Click and drag from the whitespace at the top left of the signature to the whitespace at the bottom right of the signature.

Safari users: Please use another browser for copying the signature. Chrome is known to work well.
